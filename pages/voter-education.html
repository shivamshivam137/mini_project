Knowledge is Power

Understanding the tools of democracy ensures fair and transparent elections.

EVM (Electronic Voting Machine)

EVMs are standalone machines not connected to the internet, preventing remote hacking. They have a Control Unit (for the officer) and a Balloting Unit (for you).

Watch Demo Video

VVPAT (Paper Audit Trail)

VVPAT allows you to verify that your vote was cast correctly. It prints a slip with the candidate's name, serial number, and symbol, visible for 7 seconds.

Myths vs. Facts

MYTH: My vote doesn't count.

FACT: Many elections are decided by a handful of votes. In local bodies, a single vote can change the winner.

MYTH: EVMs can be hacked via Bluetooth.

FACT: EVMs do not have any wireless communication modules (WiFi, Bluetooth, RF). They are completely isolated calculators.

MYTH: If I press 'NOTA', it cancels the election.

FACT: NOTA (None of the Above) records your dissatisfaction but does not impact the winner's result in current laws.
